Our Side of the Story

Achievers Wealth Academy LLC is a life insurance brokerage that has, in a short time, licensed 60+ agents and served 950+ clients across the country. We are committed to helping individuals and families understand how money works, so they can build lasting generational wealth the way the Rockefellers did.

We're leading a financial crusade to teach people how to fund themselves, instead of turning to GoFundMe, by using life insurance as a powerful tool for wealth creation and legacy building.

We don't just sell policies. We build leaders. Whether you're looking to protect your loved ones, grow your business, or become a licensed agent yourself, we're here to guide you every step of the way.

About the CEO

CherryJane Okeke, Founder & CEO.

From humble beginnings to industry leadership, CherryJane Okeke founded Achievers Wealth Academy LLC right in the middle of COVID, leaving behind the traditional 9 to 5 to become a licensed life insurance agent and now a Broker. She built a thriving team and has helped 2,500+ individuals secure their financial future.

With a background in Public Administration (B.Sc.) and Geriatrics (MSN), CherryJane transitioned into financial services with one mission in mind: to break the cycle of financial struggle and create a movement rooted in education, empowerment, and legacy protection.

"Life Insurance is not just for death benefits. It is a powerful tool you can use to leave a legacy."
